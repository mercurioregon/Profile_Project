I entered the world of software engineering late in life. I grew up in Central Florida but after hurricanes and insanity, I moved my family to Oregon in 2005. Part of my job is traveling the Pacific Northwest, where I have taken hundreds of photos of the natural beauty of the area. I write in my spare time. I keep a blog and I have written eight novels that are for sale. Feel free to follow my project links.
